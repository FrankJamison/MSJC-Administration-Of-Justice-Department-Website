AJ 127 Defensive Tactics for Public Safety Personnel 3 Units

Description: This course provides the student with the basic skills and knowledge to adequately defend themselves from attack and injury while in the course of their duties. The course stresses effective, proven defensive techniques and methods for weaponless defense and control of aggressive subjects. Transfers to CSU only.

Prerequisite: Students should be either entry level or in-service police, fire, or EMS personnel. All students MUST be in good physical condition and able to participate in demanding physical training.

AJ 103 Criminal Evidence 3 Units

Description: This course provides fundamental information about the rules governing the admissibility of evidence in court, including rules of evidence, presumptions and inferences; character or reputation, proof of other acts and offenses; hearsay evidence; statements; admissions and confessions, conspiracy; documentary and best secondary evidence. Includes the identification of evidence in criminal cases and the collection and preservation of evidence. Transfers to CSU only.

Prerequisite: None.

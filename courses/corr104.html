CORR 104 Legal Aspects of Corrections 3 Units

Description: This course provides students with an awareness of the historical framework, concepts, and precedents that guide correctional practice. Course material will broaden the individual's perspective of the corrections environment, the civil rights of prisoners, and responsibilities and liabilities of corrections staff. This course is a core course in the Administration of Justice Corrections Certificate. Transfers to CSU only.

Prerequisite: None.

Recommended Preparation: AJ 101.

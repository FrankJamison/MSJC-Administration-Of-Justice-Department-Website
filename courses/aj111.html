AJ 111 Administration of Justice 3 Units

Description: This course is a review of court systems; procedures from incident to final disposition; principles of constitutional, federal, state and civil laws as they apply to and affect law enforcement; included are judicial systems; bail; extradition and rendition; voir dire; direct, cross, redirect, recross, examination; rebuttal, judge's charge and instruction to the jury; writs; motions, appeals; jeopardy; clemency; and sentencing. This course is a core course in the Administration of Justice Certificate. Transfers to CSU only.

Prerequisite: None.

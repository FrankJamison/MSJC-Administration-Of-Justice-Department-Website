AJ 128 Traffic Control, Enforcement, and Investigation 3 Units

Description: This course is designed to prepare the student to understand the basic concept of traffic flow; traffic index and control; the enforcement and factors contributing to the problem; the causational factors of accidents and their investigation; the proper scene management of traffic accidents, relevant factors of prevention, the study of vehicle code laws, their inventory, storage and seizure. Transfers to CSU only.

Prerequisite: None.
